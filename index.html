ABOUT US

Organic Foods and Café is a family-run organic supermarket and cafe in the UAE, dedicated to providing a wide range of organic, biodynamic, and natural products. They prioritize quality and direct sourcing from family-run farms, ensuring hormone and chemical-free options. The company was founded by Nils El Accad after a personal health journey led him to discover the benefits of organic food. Organic Foods and Café emphasizes its family-run nature, both in its own operations and in its relationships with the farms it partners with. They source products directly from farmers, visiting them regularly and building strong relationships. They avoid buying from the open market or traders. The company offers a diverse selection of organic and biodynamic products, including fresh produce, supplements, skincare, baby items, and household products. Driven by a desire to provide healthy, nutritious food, Organic Foods and Café aims to make a real difference in the UAE's food industry. Nils El Accad's personal experience with health issues and the discovery of food additives as a cause of his illness led him to create Organic Foods and Café.
